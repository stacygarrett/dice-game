Game Rules:

  1. The game has 2 players, playing in rounds.
  2. For each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he chooses. Each roll is added to the Round score.
  3. If the player rolls a 1, the ROUND score resets to 0 and it's the next player's turn.
  4. The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his Round score gets added to the Final score.
  5. The first player to reach 100 points wins the game!
  6. Click 'Modified' to play a more advanced version.
